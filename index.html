What is Age Of Empires 3?

Age Of Empires 3 is a real-time strategy game was made by Ensemble Studios and published by Microsoft Game Studios in 2005.

AOE3 was relesed for Microsoft Windows, but the game was ported for MacOS my MacSoft it was relesed in 2006 for Macs.

There are 2 DLCs that where made for it, that being: Age Of Empires 3: The Asian Dynasties and Age of Empires 3: The WarChiefs.


Age Of Empires 3 is getting a Remake

Age Of Empires 3: Definitive Edition is a remake of the original game.

Aoe3 Definitive Edition has remasterd graphics in 4k.

It also has 2 New Civilasations, The Sweeds and The Incas.

The Remaster also has 2 new game modes Historical Battles and Art of War.
